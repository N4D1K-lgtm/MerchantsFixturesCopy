Design Views

This is the two-dimensional view where you will drop cabinets onto the grid and rearrange to meet your design needs.

Once an object is on the grind, you can drag it around to change its location as well as rotate the object to change its orientation.
After you have added fixtures to the 2D View, click the 3D View to see what the design will look like. You can click and drag the screen to change the angle of the view (up/down, left/right)

Fixtures

Cilck on the categories in this section will display all of the available fixtures. When you find a fixture you would like to add to your design, Click the fixture to select it, then click the grid in the 2D view to add it to your design. You can then click & drag the item around to place where you would like it to be in your design.

While a fixture is selected, you can also change the "Top" & "Body" properties on the right side column to change the materials.

Also, while a fixture is selected, you will cee a circle surrounding the fixture. If click & drag on the dot on this circle, you can rotate the object to change its orientaion.

To remove a ficture, select the item and then click the "delete" key on your keyboard and it will dissappear.
